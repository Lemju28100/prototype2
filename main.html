Payment Forgiveness.

Students whose outstanding term balance is $150.00 or less will not be dropped for nonpayment. They will be held in their classes and billed for the remaining balance.

How To Pay

In addition to cash, check or credit card, payment can include the application of approved financial aid, enrollment in the college's tuition payment plan or the submission of third-party payment agreements through entities such as the VA or an employer.
